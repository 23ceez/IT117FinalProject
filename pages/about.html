About Me

This is a personal introduction to Cesar's Review Journal!

Hey, I'm Cesar, the creator of this blog. Throughout my life, I've loved the stories that we share with others and the impacts those stories can have on us, on an individual level. One thing that's always fascinated me is that these stories can connect with anyone, and can foster community where you least expect it. I created this website in order to share my love for these mediums with everyone! I hope you all enjoy.
