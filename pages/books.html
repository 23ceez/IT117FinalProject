Absolute Martian Manhunter

Absolute Martian Manhunter is another installment in DC's new Absolute line of comics that combines the genius writing of Deniz Camp with extremely experimental and visually stunning art by Javier Rodriguez. Currently running on a hiatus at issue #6, the book's story is already extremely compelling. The story's selling point is the dynamic between the titular Martian Manhunter and our protagonist FBI agent John Jones, as they battle for space in Jones' mind, while Jones attempts to keep his marriage happy in a town that is in grave danger.

In conclusion, Absolute Martian Manhunter (and mostly every other Absolute series) offers a breath of fresh air into a character that has often been overlooked, with a unique blend of storytelling, art, and themes that challenge traditional comic book norms.

Score: 8/10
