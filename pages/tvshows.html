Peacemaker: Season 2

Peacemaker: Season 2 continues the story of the unconventional superhero with a mix of action, humor, and emotional depth that keeps viewers engaged. John Cena reprises his role as Chris Smith, making sure that this season's performances are more emotionally charged than ever, delivering some of his best performances as an actor yet.

In conclusion, Peacemaker: Season 2 offers a compelling continuation of the series, blending intense action sequences with character-driven storytelling that resonates with fans, but the season's pacing was totally out of whack. Sometimes it felt like the story moves at a million miles per hour, and then at a snail's pace the next episode.

Score: 8/10
