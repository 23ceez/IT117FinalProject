Cyberpunk 2077: 2 Years Later

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open world video game by CD Projekt Red that explores a dystopian cyberpunk future, featuring a star-studded cast including the likes of Keanu Reeves and Idris Elba. Initially, Cyberpunk 2077 faced criticism for releasing in an "unfinished" state, in which the game was rife with game-breaking bugs that would ruin the experience for many players. As this time has passed, CD Projekt Red has been hard at work releasing patches and updates to improve the game's stability and overall experience, and even a new DLC known as Phantom Liberty. Despite the gameplay, the narrative was always a strength, and was, for the longest time, the only reason you should play this game. But now, CD Projekt Red has addressed many of these issues, making the game a much more polished and enjoyable experience.

In conclusion, Cyberpunk 2077 has undergone significant improvements since its initial release, addressing many of the bugs and performance issues that plagued it. The game now offers a richer and more immersive experience, making it a worthwhile play for fans of the genre. I truly believe that this is one of the greatest video games of the modern era, and now that the game is not just playable, but super enjoyable to play, it is an IRREPLACEABLE experience.

Score: 10/10
